Frequently Asked Questions
How can I withdraw money?

Go to the Withdraw tab to withdraw money through bKash, Nagad or USDT. Minimum withdrawal amount is 1 USDT.

How does referral earning work?

When someone registers using your referral link, you will get a 0.01 USDT bonus. When they earn by watching ads, you will also get a percentage of their earnings.

How long does it take to get reward after watching an ad?

After watching an ad, the reward is usually added to your account within 1-2 minutes. If it doesn't arrive after 5 minutes, please contact support.

How long does withdrawal take?

After requesting a withdrawal, you will usually receive the money within 24-48 hours. It may take longer on bank holidays.
